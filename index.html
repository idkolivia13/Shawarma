Shawarma

Ingredients

    • 1 white cabbage;shredded
    • A pack of sausages
    • Chicken breast;shredded and stir-fried
    • Mayonnaise
    • Ketchup
    • A few pinches of cameroon pepper
    • Tortilla bread or shawarma bread

Instructions

1.Fry your sausages and chicken breasts seperately then plate.

2.In another bowl mix in your mayonnaise and ketchup to form a paste.Add the cameroon pepper to the paste for a little spice.The cabbage should be washed and shredded thinly.

3.Whisk the mustard, mayo, yogurt and vinegar.On a clean surface you spread out your tortilla bread.Then you spread your paste on it.Afterwards you sprinkle your cabbage on it,place your sausages and chicken breast neatly.Add a ittle more of your paste before wrapping the tortilla.

4.Repeat step 3 for all your tortilla bread.For heating it's adviced to cook for at least 5-10 minutes until lightly crisp.
